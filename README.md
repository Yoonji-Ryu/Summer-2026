Welcome! You are about to play the survival story of a teenager who has lived an eventful life. 
Every choice you make will change your destiny.

## 🏫 Chapter 1: New Country, New Life

You are a 9th-grade student living in South Korea. You are pretty good at your schoolwork and practically guaranteed to get into a prestigious high school if you keep working hard. 

Suddenly, out of nowhere, your dad drops a bombshell: 
*"We are moving to America for 2 years. Pack your bags."*

Your mind goes blank. *'What? Moving to America? What about my high school? What do I do after I come back?'*

### 🚨 What do you do?
* [1. "No, I'm not giving up on all my hard work!" - Reject the idea and stay in Korea.](chapter1_stay.md)
* [2. "America? Let's go!" - Get excited and dream of a dazzling future in the US.](chapter2.md)
